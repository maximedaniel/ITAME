Inspiration

Cairns are human-made stack of stones erected as 360°-visible landmarks and memorials since neolithic.

Design

CairnFORM is a shape-changing display that can change its revolution symmetry thanks to a modular stack of expandable illuminated rings.

Application

Different applications are possible with a shape-changing and color-changing cairn such as a light, a notifier, an hourglass, or a ring chart.

Physical charts such as physical ring charts have a strong potential for fostering public engagement through exhibitions in collective spaces (e.g. workplaces) and public spaces (e.g. train station).

Case study

In collective and public spaces such as workplaces, people come from and go to different directions. We propose to deploy CairnFORM in the middle of a openplan for helping employees to shift laptop battery charges to peak hours of local renewable energy production.

Using CairnFORM, we display a ring chart visualizing 360°-readable forecast data about local renewable energy production.
