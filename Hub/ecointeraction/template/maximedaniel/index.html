Biography

From February to July 2015, I did a research internship with Immersion (Bordeaux, France), a company specialized in Augmented and Virtual Reality technologies. During this internship, I was led to do scientific research with Potioc, an Inria research team (Bordeaux, France) in Human Computer Interaction (HCI). Thanks to Potioc and Immersion, I was able to explore some HCI fields such as Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Tangible User Interfaces (TUIs) and even Brain-Computer Interface (BCI).

Since October 2015, I am a PhD student in Computer Science at University of Bordeaux (LaBRI, Bordeaux, France) and ESTIA (Estia Research, Bidart, France). I study HCI and more precisely the applications of Tangible User Interfaces (TUIs) to Persuasive Technology (PT).
